Key Historical Events

The Renaissance

The Renaissance was a period of great cultural and intellectual revival in Europe during the 14th to the 17th centuries. It marked the rediscovery of classical philosophy, literature, and art.

The Industrial Revolution

Taking place during the 18th and 19th centuries, the Industrial Revolution transformed economies from agrarian to industrial, leading to significant technological advancements and social change.

World War II

World War II, fought from 1939 to 1945, was a global conflict that reshaped the political and social landscape of the world. It involved many nations and resulted in major geopolitical changes.
